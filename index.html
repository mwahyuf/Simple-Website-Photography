About

Scenery is a word for how a place looks, especially a beatiful, outdoorsy place. If you go to a place with mountain, beatiful trees, and gorgeous skies, then it's got great scenery. Scenery is the stuff you can look at the outside. If a place is plain, ugly, or desolate then you might say "Not much scenery".
